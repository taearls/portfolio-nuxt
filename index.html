About Me

I love learning. As a worker, I am always attracted to a dynamic environment where I will continue to grow. At my previous job, after mastering the responsibilities of my position, I stopped growing, and it dawned on me that my ambition extends far beyond the static path I was on.

Several months later, after studying and falling in love with coding in the depths of YouTube, I decided to formalize my education at General Assembly's Web Development Immersive, where I was challenged to learn the best practices of fullstack developing, as well as generate a portfolio through my own projects.

Even before diving into coding, I always gravitated towards work that applies my creativity, which is why I previously studied music, when I earned a bachelor's degree in Commercial Music from Millikin University. As a musician, the most valuable skill I've learned is the self-discipline to define goals, overcome failures, and keep moving forward towards achieving them. This discipline also applies to the ever-changing landscape of the internet and the dynamic code operating it.
